# RUSHEXA

> Prediction Markets Without Borders.

Rushexa is a unified prediction market trading protocol that connects multiple event trading platforms through a single Solana wallet. Trade across Polymarket, Kalshi, Limitless, and other supported venues without managing multiple wallets, bridges, or fragmented liquidity.

Built for traders, researchers, and institutions, Rushexa simplifies cross-platform prediction trading into one seamless experience.

---

# 🚀 What is Rushexa?

Prediction markets have become one of the fastest-growing sectors in finance, but liquidity and users remain fragmented across multiple platforms.

Each platform has its own wallet requirements, funding methods, interfaces, and order books.

Rushexa removes those barriers by providing a unified execution layer that routes orders across multiple prediction markets from one Solana wallet. Cross-platform prediction market aggregators have emerged to address fragmented liquidity by offering unified routing and execution across venues such as Polymarket, Kalshi, and Limitless.

```text
Connect Solana Wallet
          ↓
Browse All Markets
          ↓
Compare Prices
          ↓
Best Route Selected
          ↓
Execute Order
          ↓
Track Unified Portfolio
```

No bridges.

No multiple accounts.

No fragmented liquidity.
